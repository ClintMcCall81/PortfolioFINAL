Future of Information Design Jam
Future of Information Design Jam

Description

This was my first time doing a design jam. It really was exicting to put my newer skills on the line. To prove to myself that im improving. The design turned out much better than I had hoped given the 6 hour timeframe. 2 hours was spent on brainstorming and finding our "users", The other 4 hours was spent designing and completing the presentation slides for our concept. Our team consisted of 6 people Myself, Sarah Murad, Lily Phan, Alex B, Ava, and Luiz Veiga. We split the responsibilites as such: Sarah, Myself, and Lily handled the UI Design, Wireframing, and Sitemaps. The other half of the teamwork handled the User Journeys, User Stories, and Personas.
