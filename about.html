About Me

Who I Am

Hi! My name is Clint McCall, Im a avid gamer and inspiring UI Designer. I currently live in Toronto where I currently study Interaction Design at George Brown College. I have a long history with design (since highschool) back when I took Cyber Arts at Northview Heights Secondary School and have always wanted to pursue my dream of becoming a Game Designer / Level Designer at Ubisoft Toronto however due to difficulties I had to switch studys to my current field of study. This however hasnt swayed me from pursuing my dream of game design in general. Im still learning and trying my hardest to build a portfolio worthy of getting me into the game design industry.

My Inspiration

As previously mentioned I am a avid gamer. I play lots of games on my spare time, mostly to take breaks between assignments, but also to gain insperation from other developers on how they desinged there games unique user interfaces all the way down from the HUDS, GUIS, etc... Its tons of fun to see how they fit information of the player into well made graphical elements. Being able to think as the player is something ive been trying to hone my skills for when it comes to game design, and to be completely honest I think of Interaction Design the same way I think of games and there interfaces. Its what keeps me learning about the theory and thought behind the elements on the screen!
